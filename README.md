## 🔍 What is Pegasus Spyware?

**Pegasus spyware** is the world's most advanced **mobile surveillance malware**, created by Israeli cyber-intelligence firm **NSO Group** in 2010. This **military-grade spyware** can completely compromise iPhones and Android devices without the victim's knowledge using **zero-click exploits**.

### Why Pegasus Spyware is Dangerous

| Threat Level | Impact | Detection Difficulty |
|-------------|--------|---------------------|
| **Critical** | Total device takeover | Nearly invisible |

**Pegasus spyware** exploits **zero-day vulnerabilities** in iOS and Android to gain root access, enabling 24/7 surveillance of calls, messages, camera, microphone, and location data.

### Pegasus Spyware Quick Facts

- **Developer:** NSO Group (Israel)
- **First Seen:** 2016
- **Platforms:** iOS, Android, BlackBerry, Symbian
- **Infection Method:** Zero-click exploits, spear phishing
- **Cost:** $650,000+ for 10 licenses
- **Status:** **Active threat in 2025-2026**

> ⚠️ **Critical:** Pegasus spyware requires **NO user interaction** to infect devices. Simply receiving a message can trigger infection.
